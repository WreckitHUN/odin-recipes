Pancake

Ingredients

  • 125 g all purpose flour
  • 2 eggs (nagy)
  • 20 g vanillin sugar
  • A pinch of salt
  • 250 ml milk (cold)
  • 40 g butter (melted)
  • 6 tbsp sunflower oil

How to make?

  • Put the flour, eggs, vanilla sugar, and salt into a mixing bowl.

  • Start mixing with a mixer, then gradually add the milk and finally the melted butter. Keep mixing until you get a smooth pancake batter.

  • Cover it and let it rest in the refrigerator for 2 hours. The batter is good when its consistency is similar to cooking cream. (If necessary, you can thin it with a little milk.)

    Heat up your Teflon pancake pan. (Tip: I usually spread a few drops of oil thinly with a brush before frying each pancake.)

    Take the pan off the heat, and ladle in the batter (about half a ladleful). Tilt and rotate the pancake pan to spread the batter evenly and thinly at the bottom of the pan.

    Fry the pancake over medium heat for 45-60 seconds, or until it separates from the pan. Loosen the pancake, flip it over, and fry the other side for about 30 seconds.

    Transfer it to a plate. Stack the finished pancakes on top of each other, and cover them with a kitchen towel until you fry all of them.
